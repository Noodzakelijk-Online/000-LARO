Key Features

🧠

AI-Powered Case Matching

Our platform employs sophisticated artificial intelligence to analyze your circumstances and automatically determine the appropriate legal field(s). Using natural language processing and machine learning, it ensures accurate case classification, eliminating the need to decipher complex legal terminology.

📄

Automated Data Aggregation

The system seamlessly integrates with email platforms and cloud storage solutions, consolidating all relevant documents and communications into a structured evidence trail. It provides a summarized "red line" thread of your case for legal professionals, eliminating manual compilation while ensuring lawyers receive a concise case summary.

✉️

Intelligent Outreach & Follow-Ups

Our service automates email outreach to legal professionals throughout the Netherlands registered in the Nederlandse orde van advocaten (NOvA), focusing on those with expertise in the relevant legal field(s). It monitors responses and sends courteous follow-up messages until a lawyer either accepts or declines your case.

How It Works

1
💬

Describe Your Case

Simply explain your legal situation in your own words. Our AI will analyze your description to identify the appropriate legal field(s) and determine the complexity of your case.

2
🔗

Connect Your Accounts

Securely connect your email (Gmail/Outlook) and cloud storage (Google Drive/OneDrive) accounts, or manually upload relevant documents. Our system will organize everything into a structured case file.

3
📨

AI-Powered Outreach

Our platform automatically contacts qualified lawyers specializing in your case's legal field(s). It handles all follow-up communications until a lawyer accepts your case.

4
🤝

Connect With Your Lawyer

Once a lawyer accepts your case, we facilitate the introduction and provide them with your complete case file, including the organized evidence trail and case summary.
