Platform Interface Overview

This document provides screenshots of the key interfaces of the Legal AI Reach Out platform, demonstrating how the platform looks and functions without requiring a live deployment.

Home Page

The landing page introduces users to the Legal AI Reach Out platform, explaining its purpose and key features. It includes a prominent call-to-action for users to get started with their legal case.

Key Elements:

  • Platform introduction and value proposition
  • Overview of the AI-powered case matching system
  • Explanation of the document aggregation capabilities
  • Description of the automated lawyer outreach process
  • Call-to-action buttons for registration and login

User Dashboard

The user dashboard provides a comprehensive interface for users to manage their legal cases, view documents, and track lawyer responses. It offers a clear overview of case progress and resource usage.

Key Elements:

  • Active case summary with current status
  • Case progress tracker showing completed and pending steps
  • Lawyer response section displaying matched lawyers and their responses
  • Document management area for viewing and organizing case documents
  • Resource usage and billing information based on the pay-per-use model
  • AI case analysis showing identified legal fields and case complexity

Investor Authentication

The investor authentication page provides a secure gateway for investors to access the business metrics dashboard. Investors need to provide their email address to gain access to the platform's performance data.

Key Elements:

  • Email authentication form
  • Brief explanation of the investor dashboard
  • Security notice about data protection
  • Link to contact support for access issues

Investor Dashboard

The investor dashboard provides comprehensive insights into the platform's performance metrics, business plan assumptions, and real-time data. It allows investors to track key performance indicators and compare actual results against projections.

Key Elements:

  • Key performance indicators (response rates, case acceptance, time to lawyer, profit margin)
  • Performance metrics with historical trends and comparisons against targets
  • Business plan assumptions section showing market and business model assumptions
  • Financial projections comparing projected vs. actual performance
  • Impact goals progress tracking
  • Real-time data updates with visual indicators

Business Assumptions

The business assumptions section of the investor dashboard displays the core assumptions from the business plan, allowing investors to understand the foundation of the business model and compare actual performance against these assumptions.

Key Elements:

  • Market assumptions (market size, lawyer workload, initial response rate, case acceptance rate)
  • Business model assumptions (revenue model, AI processing cost, storage cost, email outreach cost)
  • Clear presentation of values and descriptions for each assumption
